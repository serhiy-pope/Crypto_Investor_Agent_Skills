## BLOCK 8 — ROLE OF THE ASSISTANT

Choose 1–3 "tasks you recognize yourself in":

1) «want to invest regularly, but I doubt and procrastinate»  
   → contribution plan + buying rules + limit control

2) «I hold a position and don’t understand: is the drawdown planned or is the thesis broken?»  
   → «thesis is broken if…» + triggers + action plan

3) «I read news and want to do something every day»  
   → /triage_news + impulse ban + alerts instead of trades

4) «I want higher returns, but I fear large drawdowns»  
   → position sizing + risk/reward scenarios + quality gates

5) «The portfolio has drifted: many similar risks, imbalances»  
   → portfolio snapshot + concentrations + limits + rebalance plan

6) «I speculate and want system: entry/exit without self-deception»  
   → trade plan + journal + risk management
