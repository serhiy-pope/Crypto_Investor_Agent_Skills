## SECTION A — UNBREAKABLE PRINCIPLES

If an idea violates even one principle → immediate rejection, no discussion.

**A.1. Anti-Ruin / Sovereignty of Decisions**
Any positions where they can force me to act are prohibited: liquidation by exchange, forced margin call, uncovered obligations. A single point of failure (one asset / counterparty / exchange / wallet) capable of destroying the portfolio is forbidden.

**A.2. Validation, not emotions**
There is no absolute certainty in crypto. I resolve doubts with a pilot position + addition rules — not with conviction or community hype.

**A.3. Network/Protocol value mindset**
I buy network effects, protocol revenue, and token utility — not price action and narratives alone. Quality comes first: "hot narrative" does not justify "bad tokenomics."

**A.4. System + structural resilience**
Fundamentals = WHAT to buy. Market = WHEN. Plan = HOW MUCH. The future is unpredictable — I build a portfolio for all cycle scenarios, not just one (bull only).

**A.5. Risk ≠ volatility**
Fatal risk (critical portfolio damage, -80% on core) is unacceptable. Operational loss (controlled trade loss with stop) is acceptable as a working expense. I let winners grow, cut losses quickly if the thesis is violated.

**A.6. Execution discipline**
A correct but too-early entry ≈ wrong. 90% of control lies in risks and stops. No restructuring based on tweets or emotions. Every action only via predefined triggers.

**A.7. Custody first**
Every position decision includes custody consideration. "Not your keys, not your coins" — core holdings must have a self-custody plan.

## SECTION B — TRADE ADMISSION FILTER (quick pre-screen)

The transaction is prohibited if at least one answer is NO. "Not quite YES" = NO.

| # | Question | Criterion |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **I understand** | I can explain in 2 minutes: what I'm buying, value source, 3 main risks |
| 2 | **No coercion** | No leverage, no liquidation scenarios, no uncovered positions |
| 3 | **Liquid** | I can close within 24h at ≤ 5% price impact (CEX or DEX) |
| 4 | **I can withstand it** | −70% stress does not break me financially or psychologically |
| 5 | **Custody ready** | I have a custody plan before entering (exchange / wallet allocation) |

Filter passed → proceed to 0-Gate and QPTA.

## SECTION C — ASSET CLASSES (GREEN / YELLOW / ORANGE / RED)

### GREEN — Core / Long-term Conviction
- **What qualifies:** BTC, ETH, top L1s with proven network effects and 4+ year track record
- Position limit: ≤ `[15]`% per asset (drift up to `[20]`% allowed due to growth; above — rebalance)
- Exception: BTC as reserve asset — up to 50% within regime limits
- Risk management: not stop-loss, but fundamental/tokenomics invalidation
- Entry rules: only BUY or STRONG BUY. In euphoria regime — buying is prohibited.
- Custody: minimum 80% of GREEN positions in cold storage

**2 levels of protection instead of stop-loss:**

Level 1 — Fundamental stop (trigger-checklist mandatory):
```
✓ Network effect/activity declining for ___ consecutive months  [example: 3 months]
✓ Developer activity collapse (GitHub commits)
✓ Protocol revenue / fee collapse
✓ Tokenomics deterioration (excessive inflation, massive unlocks)
✓ Regulatory prohibition in key jurisdictions
```

Level 2 — Technical Risk-Off (on SELL / STRONG SELL):
```
→ Stop adding
→ Reduce position to lower bound of target range
→ Partial conversion to stablecoin/BTC until BUY returns
```

### YELLOW — Protocol Trades / Narrative Plays / Mid-term
- **What qualifies:** Established DeFi protocols, L2s, sector narratives, altcoins with clear thesis
- Position limit: ≤ `[5]`% per asset (drift up to `[8]`%)
- Risk per idea: 1R = `[1.0]`% capital (standard) / `[0.5]`% (Impulse BUY)
- Risk management: **stop-loss mandatory** (in USD terms, not just % from entry)
- No averaging down without predefined rule

### ORANGE — Asymmetry / Speculative / Early-stage
- Hard limit: ≤ `[2]`% per position
- UsedRisk ≤ `[0.5]`% per idea
- Convexity mandatory: Profit/Loss ≥ 5:1 (higher threshold than stocks due to binary outcomes)
- Goal: asymmetry and high-conviction small bets, not "casino"
- Required: smart contract audit passed OR explicit risk acceptance recorded

### RED — Prohibited
0%. No trades:
- Leveraged tokens (2x/3x/5x) without explicit constitution exception
- Unaudited smart contracts (protocols < 6 months old without audit)
- OTC/P2P for investment (only for fiat on/off-ramp)
- Anonymous team protocols with no doxxed developers
- Margin/futures without explicit Risk Manager approval in journal

## SECTION E — QPTA GATE

**QPTA = Quality / Price / Timing / Asymmetry**

| Gate | Question |
|---|---|
| **Q** (Quality) | Has the asset passed tokenomics, team, and on-chain quality filter? |
| **P** (Price) | Is the price in acceptable zone? (not in euphoria / not at market cycle top) |
| **T** (Timing) | Does BTC cycle, market regime, and trend allow entry? |
| **A** (Asymmetry) | Upside/Downside ≥ 3:1? Size within Heat limits? |

Rule: **all four must be YES**. One NO → trade **not allowed**.

*Example (educational case):*
```
Asset: ETH | Class: GREEN
Q: Established network, deflationary issuance, staking yield, EIP-1559 → YES
P: P/ETH market cycle position mid-cycle, not at ATH zone → YES
T: BTC dominance trend down (altcoin season starting), Trend Score ≥ 6 → YES
A: Bear scenario -30%, Bull scenario +120%; ratio ≥ 4:1 → YES
→ DECISION: EXECUTE (pilot position)
```

## SECTION F — POSITION SIZE CALCULATION

### YELLOW (altcoins/protocols with stop)
```
Stop% = distance to stop in %
Position% = min(Class limit%, R / Stop%)
UsedRisk% = Position% × Stop%
Admission condition: UsedRisk% ≤ R
```

*Example: SOL ecosystem token, YELLOW, R=1%, Stop%=15%*
*Position% = min(5%, 1%/15%) = min(5%, 6.7%) = 5%*
*UsedRisk% = 5% × 15% = 0.75% ✓ (≤ 1%)*

### ORANGE (speculative, binary risk)
```
UsedRisk% = entry size% (full loss assumed)
Condition: UsedRisk% ≤ 0.5%
```

*Example: New DeFi protocol, ORANGE, entry 0.5% portfolio → UsedRisk% = 0.5% → OK*

```

Risk managed via fundamental triggers and portfolio limits
Cold storage: ≥ 80% of GREEN position weight
```

## SECTION G — PYRAMIDING AND ADDING

- Start with pilot when uncertain — market must pay for risk
- Pilot hits −1R → close (thesis/timing not confirmed)
- Pilot in loss, thesis alive, filters intact → hold, but **do not increase**

**YELLOW adding rule:**
Only at price above entry OR at breakout of confirming level (EMA50 / weekly close above key resistance).

**GREEN adding rule:**
In V_low zone (undervaluation by on-chain metrics), if tokenomics unchanged, regime allows exposure, Trend permits.
**Forbidden** to average if protocol revenue declines >30% or tokenomics deteriorates.

## SECTION H — TIMING: TREND SCORE

### Algorithm (EMA 10/20/50/90/200, ATR(14)) — same as equities, applied to daily charts

**Squeeze:** |EMA10 − EMA200| < 1.2 × ATR → status HOLD, no new buys.

**Scoring:** for each EMA: +1 if Price > EMA, −1 if below. +1 if slope up, −1 down. Sum = Trend Score.

| Score | Status | Allowed actions |
|---|---|---|
| ≥ 6 and Price > EMA200 | STRONG BUY | Full throttle within limits |
| ≥ 4 | BUY | Cautious entry |
| < 4 or Squeeze | HOLD | Hold only, no buying |
| ≤ −4 | SELL / STRONG SELL | Defense, reduce risk |

### BTC Cycle Context (additional filter for GREEN/YELLOW)
| BTC Cycle Position | GREEN action | YELLOW action |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-halving / Accumulation | Accumulate pilots | Small YELLOW, tight stops |
| Halving year / Early bull | Add to GREEN | Open YELLOW positions |
| Mid bull (6–12m after halving) | Hold GREEN, trim at extremes | Active YELLOW, higher Heat |
| Late bull / Euphoria | Stop adding GREEN | Close YELLOW, reduce Heat |
| Bear market | Hold GREEN core only | No YELLOW entries |

## SECTION O — DRAWDOWN AUTOPILOT

| Drawdown from peak | Actions |
|---|---|
| **−20%** | No changes, rule monitoring only |
| **−35%** | Pause new YELLOW/ORANGE. Reduce Directional Heat by 30–50%. Correlation audit |
| **−50%** | Audit "persistent losses". Forced exposure reduction. Ban scaling "by narrative" |
| **−70% (Red button)** | Survival threat → reduce risk / move to stablecoins/BTC. Post-analysis after |

Rule: **in bad regime we cut risks, not "revenge trade."**

**Crypto note:** bear markets routinely reach −80% from ATH on BTC and −90%+ on altcoins. Autopilot thresholds are set higher than equities because crypto volatility is structurally higher.

## APPENDIX — EXCEPTION PROTOCOL

**Violation** — deviation for "returns / opinion / emotion".
**Authorized deviation** — external constraint / force majeure; goal = risk control and reduction, NOT profit.

Allowed exceptions (closed list):
- Exchange maintenance / withdrawal freeze (custody emergency)
- Regulatory action / jurisdiction change
- Protocol hack / exploit (exit immediately without journal approval)
- Red button (survival threat)

NOT valid reasons: "I think…", tweets, "don't want to realize loss", "need to recoup my losses", "everyone is buying", "the community said so".

**Master-rule:** an exception CANNOT increase tail risk.
